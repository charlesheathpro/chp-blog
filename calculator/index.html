What Affects Your Premium?

🏦
Mortgage Balance Your coverage amount is typically set to match your remaining mortgage — so your family's home is fully protected.
🎂
Age The single biggest driver of premium. Locking in coverage while you're younger means significantly lower rates for the entire term.
❤️
Health Class Carriers assign health classifications based on your medical history. Preferred health can save 20–40% compared to a standard rating.
🚬
Tobacco Use Smokers typically pay 2–3× the non-smoker rate. Quitting for 12+ months can qualify you for non-smoker pricing at many carriers.
📅
Term Length Match your coverage term to your mortgage payoff date. A 30-year term costs more than a 15-year term but ensures full protection throughout.
